Tic-Tac-Toe Game Rules

1. Game Setup:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Players take turns, and the first move alternates between Player 1 and Player 2 at the start of each game.
  • Each player is assigned a symbol ("X" or "O") at the beginning of each game.

2. Making a Move:

  • Players tap on an empty cell to place their symbol on the grid during their turn.

3. Objective:

  • Form a line of three of your symbols ("X" or "O") horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win the game.

4. Scoring:

  • A player receives 1 point for forming a winning line.
  • If the game ends in a draw, with no winning lines, neither player receives any points.
  • If a player achieves two winning lines simultaneously or wins from two sides (diagonal and either row or column), they receive 2 points.

5. Game End:

  • The game ends when one player forms a winning line or the entire grid is filled with symbols without a winner.

6. Restart and Score Reset:

  • Players can restart the game by tapping a "Restart" button.
  • The score can be reset to zero for both players with a "Reset Score" button.
